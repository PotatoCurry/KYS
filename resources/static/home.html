Seniors-Notification sent to Seniors

From the YES office, notifications are being sent to the students via their 3rd period teachers if a student either 1) has a substantial number of YES hours recorded but missing a YES contract; or 2) has over 75 but less than 100 YES hours accumulated so far. Please note that the YES hour submission deadline for seniors is April 12, 2019. To be rewarded a YES medal at graduation, a senior must have at least 100 YES hours in record.


Remember:

  • Always fill out your YES form completely. DO NOT LEAVE ANYTHING BLANK!!
  • Never alter hours.
  • Always bring both the original and a copy of your form to the YES table.
  • If you work more than one day or 8 hours, always bring a log.
  • Always have your log signed.
